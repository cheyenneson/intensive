We are so excited to see you this weekend!

Below is all the information you'll need to have a smooth and fun experience. Please read carefully and reach out to aspenballetutah@gmail.com if you have any questions.

Location

245 S Main St, Springville, UT

Studio 1


What to Wear

Wear anything you feel comfortable dancing in! We suggest:


Tights, leotard, and a skirt

Leggings and a tank top


Please avoid clothing with offensive writing or symbols. Be mindful of the comfort and safety of those around you.


What to Bring

Water

A snack

Warmups

Any of your normal dance gear

Pointe shoes, if you wear them


Parking & Entrance

Please park behind or on the side of the building. Look for the beautiful dance mural—parking near it is fine. Drive slowly as there will be little dancers walking to and from cars.


We will be entering and exiting through the back of the building, where Studio 1 is located. The front doors will remain locked all weekend.

Payment

A $60 fee is due upon arrival ($10 per class for drop ins). We accept Venmo or cash.

Due to the number of spouses attending, men will receive 50% off

Venmo Us Here

Saturday Showcase
At 3:30 PM on Saturday, we will have a small showcase. This is a time for dancers to support and watch one another. Close friends and family are welcome, but please limit your guests to 2 due to space limitations.


Studio Etiquette
We are guests of Charisma Dance Studio, and we ask that everyone:


  • Show respect for the space, teachers, and fellow dancers

  • Arrive and leave on time

  • Use inside voices (no yelling)

  • Do not eat in the studio (use the break room)

  • Follow the campsite rule: leave people and places better than you found them



Misconduct of any kind will result in being asked to leave the event.

 


We can’t wait to dance with you and share a beautiful weekend of movement and connection.
